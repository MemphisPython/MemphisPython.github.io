Meeting Roundup

Tonight's meeting was a little different, but there were great conversations covering many topics. Here are just a few:

  • General discussion on application stacks (with a nod to Web Operations by John Allspaw and Jesse Robbins).
  • Python's __dict__ attribute (see Python's docs on special attributes and Controlling Attribute Access).
  • One of the top-ten Common mistakes that python programmer's make.
  • @dboze's simple nearest-neighbor inpainting implementation: innpain.
  • Other image and computer vision tools: Scikit-image, SimpleCV, NumPy, and Pillow.
  • Python for music and audio and pygame.
