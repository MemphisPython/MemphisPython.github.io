Memphis Tech Talks & the June Meetup

Date Sat 11 June 2016 By Brad Montgomery Category announcements Tags python / meetings / talks

Memphis Tech Talks

On Thursday, June 16, the Memphis Technology Foundation & Fedex Institute of technology are hosting an evening of lighthing talks. This will be a great showcase of various tech topics from local professionals.

You can see the current speaker lineup over at memphistechnology.org, and you can RSVP for the event on meetup.com.

MEMpy road trip

If you can only make one #memtech event this month, it should be the Tech Talks. And since we think it'll be a spectacular evening, we're putting the regular MEMpy meeting on hold this month.

However, if you need to get your Python fix, a few of us are planning a road trip to visit PyNash on Thursday, June 23 at 6:00pm for an introduction to Django Channels.

If you'd like to come along, please RSVP at on the PyNash meetup, and get in touch with Brad / @bkmontgomery or Douglas for travel details.
